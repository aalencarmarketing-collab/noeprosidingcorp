Our Story

Craftsmanship You Can Trust

Noe Pro Siding Corp was built on a simple idea: homeowners deserve a contractor who shows up, does the work right, and stands behind it. We are a family-owned company based in Milford, MA, specializing in siding installation, window replacement, and custom deck construction for residential properties.

Over the past 10+ years, we've completed more than 500 projects across Massachusetts — from full siding replacements on multi-story homes to custom decks built for family gatherings. Every project gets the same attention to detail, whether it's a small repair or a full exterior renovation.

We speak English, Portuguese, and basic Spanish, making it easy to communicate clearly with a diverse range of homeowners across our service area.
